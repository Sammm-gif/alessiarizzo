I was born in Northern Italy in 1999.
In 2019 I enrolled at the School of Fine Arts "Accademia Albertina" in Turin.
Here, I perfected my technical skills and I came in touch with different forms of art. While I had always been painting it was a turning point for me since it allowed to experiment and enlarge my artistic capabilities.
I also had the opportunity to meet amazing artists and to even join the "Collettivo Mostro" which allowed me to play in a different regimen, without fixed hierarchies and institutional obligations.
This forced me to experiment with collective work which taught me to create strategies and synergies and share spaces with other artists.
My favourite form of art is definitely painting which has always been a huge part of my persona, but expanding my style was a priority of mine during university years so I started employing different materials and techniques, in order to give my works more substance and three-dimensionality.
What I feel the urge to express and share through my paintings are the nature and the psyche of human being. I am interested in the relationship between body and space, which is interpreted as the structure of existence.
What I usually do is rearranging elements such as people and things that are observed from my point of view. I take photos and then I expand on those with my art.
